Natural Garden Solutions


Restore Your Landscape

Natural Garden Solutions is devoted to ecological restoration. Through native plant landscaping and rooftop gardens we reduce CO2 emissions to clean our planet. The benefits of rooftop gardens include:

  • Cleaning CO2 emissions
  • Reducing heating costs in winter
  • Reducing cooling costs in the summer
  • Storm water run off retention
  • Create habitat for pollinators
  • Beautify cities

Creating a Sustainable Future

Olivia Olvera and Brady Neiberger founded this compnay in 2021 with the vision of creating a more sustainable future. When you integrate native plant species across your landscape you are helping to restore and reconnect your local ecosystem.
Thank you for helping your community lead the way to a better future!
