About

Natural Garden Solutions is an ecological restoration company based out of Wayzata, Minnesota. It all started in a boiling hot spring tucked away in the Tobacco Root mountains of SW Montana. It was a balmy -26 degrees and snowing steadily. As the founders soaked there staring off into the milky way and moon lit mountain tops in the distance they had a vision, to save the planet one plant at a time. Natural Garden Solutions goal is to reconnect urban landscapes back into their natural ecotype. We are a landscaping company who is determined to make a difference. We believe integrating native plant species across land and water will create habitat for other natural occurring organisms, as well as reintroduce natural ecological processes that have been lost. We hope to protect native species that are threatened or endangered. Conserving natural resources is what our company stands behind. Quality appealing landscaping is what we provide.
